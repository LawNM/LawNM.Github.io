Purpose of E-Weatherman

E-Weatherman's purpose is to give the end-user and accurate reading on the forecast. This will allow our consumers/audience to go get ready for and go about their day in confiedence knowing that they are able to be ready for whatever may come there way that day. E-Weatherman will give the end-users a variety of important figueres for them to use in their upcoming day, such as highs, lows, and the general weather for the day.
