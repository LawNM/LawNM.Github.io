Featured Businesses

Locksmithy

Ark: Lock & Security, got their start in 1982 when the owner John Locke found that he was abosulutly fascinated with how locks worked. It was simple thing he got some bobby pins and went to town on every pin and tumbler lock in the house. Unfourtanly after he broke the front door Lock. his parents got quite upset. So, then after a month long grounding John was given some old padlocks. After much practice he was given an appretinceship and opened his shop after graduating high school.

Paramount 5

What started as backyard Theater turned into the Paramount 5. Phil loved movies, he loved watching them and sharing them with others. So he decided that he would get a projector and show movies in his backyard. At first the only people were his immediate family. Soon it became friends and their families. What were once weekly showing became double features. Then Friday evenings and all day saturday. When Phil had saved enough money He bought a closed two screen theater. After more succusses he had the current five screen theater built.

GamePulse

When Gary Game was in school he loved to play Dungeons and Dragons and card games. It was and still is his favoriate thing to do. Sadly, he got bullied a lot. So he after he graduaterd college with a degree in Business managment he decided to open up his own gamestore. This was to be a place for all people. A place where you could show up, be yourself and just enjoy the things you enjoy without anyone to make you feel bad about it.
